### Motivations As Origins

Some characters may derive their powers from their motivation in some way, such as heroes who draw strength from  their  convictions,  faith,  or  morality. This  provides  a descriptor for those powers, but the hero may also suffer
Power  Loss  (see  page  28)  from  a  change  or  wavering  in motivation.
### Motivations In Conflict

A character with different motivations may find them in conflict from time to time. Such conflicts provide roleplaying opportunities and complications for players and story hooks for the Gamemaster. For example, a hero motivated by Patriotism may discover a secret government agency acting against the interests of justice in the world. What is stronger, the heroâ€™s patriotism or the desire to see the truth known and justice done? Some conflicts may even result  in  heroes  changing  motivations.  See  Changing
Complications, in the following section, for more on this.
