### Fast Grab
COMBAT
When you hit with an unarmed attack you can immedi-
ately make a grab check against that opponent as a free
action (see Grab, page 248). Your unarmed attack inflicts
its normal damage and counts as the initial attack check
required to grab your opponent.

### Grabbing Finesse
COMBAT
You can use your Dexterity bonus, rather than your
Strength bonus, to make grab attacks. You are not vulner-
able while grabbing. See Grab, page 248, for details. This
is a good advantage for skilled unarmed combatants fo-
cused more on speed than strength.
