### Choosing Complications

Choose at least two complications for your hero: a Motivation and at least one other. You can take as many complications  as  you  wish,  although  the  GM  may  set  limits  for the sake of being able to keep track of them all. Complications  are  also  self-limiting,  in  that  you  only  earn  hero points  for  those  complications  that  actually  come  into play.  So  even  if  you  have  more  than  a  dozen,  if  the  GM can only include a couple in a game session, then those are the ones that earn you hero points for that game. Keep  in  mind  the  adventure  needs  to  have  room for all of the heroesГўв‚¬в„ў complications, so individual ones can only come up so often.
