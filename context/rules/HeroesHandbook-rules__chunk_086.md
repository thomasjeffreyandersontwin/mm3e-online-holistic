### Opposed Effect Checks
In some cases, usually when one effect is used directly
against another, or against a particular trait like an abil-
ity or skill, an opposed check is called for (see Opposed
Checks in The Basics chapter). If a contest is entirely a
matter of whose power is greater, a comparison check
(see page 14) is called for; the character with the higher
power rank wins automatically.
