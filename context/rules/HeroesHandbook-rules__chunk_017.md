### Conditions

Heroes  run  into  their  share  of  difficulties  in  their  work.
One way MUTANTS & MASTERMINDS measures this is with different  conditions.  They  are  shorthand  for  the  different game modifiers imposed by things from power effects to injuries or fatigue.
