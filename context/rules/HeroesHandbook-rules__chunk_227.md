### Other Rewards

Although  good  deeds  are  their  own  reward,  heroes  do sometimes  receive  other  rewards  in  addition  to  power points. The Gamemaster should feel free to award any of the following to heroes according to the outcome of the adventure and the needs of the series.

There may be rewards for the capture of certain wanted super-criminals  and  grateful  governments  or  corporations  might  choose  to  reward  helpful  heroes  financially.
Heroes  often  use  these  rewards  to  help  fund  their  own efforts  (paying  for  their  equipment,  headquarters,  etc.)

Sometimes  the  monetary  rewards  of  heroism  come  in the form of ongoing support from a wealthy patron, trust fund, corporation, or government agency. They can all offer financial backing to a hero or team of heroes and provide them with the resources they need.

Heroes who come into a sudden windfall can invest some earned power points acquiring the Benefit advantage to represent  their  increased  wealth.
